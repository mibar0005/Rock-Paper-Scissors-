This is the classic game Rock, Paper, Scissors.
For this game you will play against the computer.
The program contains three random choices 1 though 3.
1 is rock.
2 is paper.
3 is scissors.
The user should enter their choice on they keyboard. 
The computer will display its choice.
Classic Rules apply:
Rock smashes scissors.
Scissors cuts papers.
Paper covers rock.
In case of a drawing the same choice, the game must be again.
Have fun!
